Junior Applied Maths Seminar (JAMS)

JAMS is the Junior Applied Mathematics Seminar run by graduate students, for graduate students! We aim to create a more relaxed environment for students to present their work and allow them to explain the current progress on their work. This also allows students to have more of a voice in post-talk discussions without more senior members dominating the discussion. There's free lunch afterwards for further discussion.

If you wish to give a talk please contact our JAMS coordinator Ian Roper.
