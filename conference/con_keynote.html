Keynote Speakers

Camille Duprat

Assistant Professor / Maître de conférences (EP/X)

  LadHyX - Ecole Polytechnique, Palaiseau, France

Camille Duprat is an Assistant Professor at Ecole Polytechnique in Paris. Her research is concerned with solid mechanics and fluid dynamics, with a focus on low Reynolds number fluid motions; those dominated by viscosity. Camille utilises both theoretical and experimental studies, and at the moment, is looking at problems involving the coupling of capillary-driven and elastic structures.

Monica Marinescu

Lecturer in Electrochemical Storage, Department of Mechanical Engineering

  Imperial College London

Monica is a lecturer in Electrochemical Storage at the Faculty of Engineering of Imperial College Lond. Her research focuses on the theoretical description of the properties of and phenomena taking place at the electrolyte-electrolyte and electrolyte-electrode interfaces, for applications such as electrowetting, electro-variable liquid lenses and mirrors, and nanoplasmonics.
