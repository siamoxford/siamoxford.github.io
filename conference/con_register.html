About the Conference

The 11th annual Oxford SIAM-IMA Student Chapter conference will be hosted on Wednesday 1st May 2019 at the Mathematical Institute of the University of Oxford.

The aim of the conference is to encourage greater interaction between the industrial and applied mathematics communities. This opportunity to meet and engage with academics from a wide range of disciplines will be of benefit, in particular, to graduate students and early career researchers.

Participation is completely free and includes food and drink throughout the day.

Student talks and posters

Contributed talks consist of a 15-minute presentation followed by a 5-minute discussion. The poster session will be 1 hour long and provides excellent opportunities for networking and in-depth discussions about research projects.

Posters may be up to A0 in size however for posters larger than A2, portrait orientation is recommended.

Please submit your abstract for a contributed talk or poster using the submission form by Monday 1st April.
